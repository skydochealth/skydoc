About SkyDoc

SkyDoc is a founder-led concept (Ayşe Melis Günal). The goal is to build a clinical-grade telemedicine platform tailored for aviation: rapid decision support, seamless clinician-to-flightdeck communication, and post-event case management. This landing page is intentionally minimal — it timestamps the idea and enables early conversations with partners and advisors.

Core features

Real-time teleconsult

Encrypted audio, video, and structured clinical forms optimized for in-flight bandwidth.

Onboard decision support

Guided checklists and severity scoring for common 112/EMS & in-flight medical events.

Incident reporting

Exportable, time-stamped incident reports for airlines, insurers, and regulators.
